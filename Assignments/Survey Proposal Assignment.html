Objectives

During this assignment you should learn:

  • How to write a proposal for a survey project

Assignment

For the past couple of years I have heard significant complaints about the MBA Career Services especially surrounding the reporting requirements and the way they encourage job searching.

Your assignment is to write a proposal for designing a survey that can be used by Career Services to improve the way they operate. This is just a proposal, you do not need to design the survey (yet)!

Your proposal should include:

  1. A formal statement of the business question being considered: You can decide on the business questions as you are more informed about the issues than I am.
  2. Relevant Details about the context or constraints on the survey
  3. Statement of expected population and plans for securing sample. This does not have to be current students.
  4. Timeline for the project
  5. Expected cost of the project. There might not be any costs depending on the population and the business problem. If there is not a cost please specify that as well.

Remember that you are not proposing solutions only studying the issues. (The time for proposal solutions will come later.)

An example proposal can be found at: Proposal Example

Deliverables

You should submit your written proposal to Canvas as a document.
