Tips and tricks

You will need to know how to compute the mean and the standard deviation to complete this assignment. The formula for the mean is: x - = ∑ i = 1 N x i N In words this means that the mean of a set of values (x with a bar over it) is the sum from 1 to the number of items in the list of each item divided by the number of items in the list.

The formula for the standard deviation is: s x = ∑ i = 1 N x i - x - N - 1 In words this means that to calculate the standard deviation of a list of values x, you need to subtract each value from the mean, sum them, and then divide by the number of items minus one.

Standardizing the values have the formula: S t a n d a r d i z e d   V a l u e = x i - x - s x

A moving average is just the mean of that item and the previous items in the window. If your window is 7, then the first 6 values will not be present in the resulting moving average since there is not 7 items to average them with.
